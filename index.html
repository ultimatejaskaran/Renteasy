About Us

“We are a group of people at IIT-Delhi, who aspire to solve existing problems in the society using our intelect and resources.”

Currently, we are working with public libraries to solve the problem of costly books in the market. Also public libraries don't benefit much due to decrease in number of people visiting the public libraires. So, we seek to provide a solution where both the public libraries and book readers benefit. Through this we make an effort to increase availability of more number and more localised books online. Our team consists of some brightst minds of IIT-D. Vaibhav Jain, Nikhil, Divyanshu , Jaskaran, Aditya and Anchal with their best knowledge bring to you this amazing books at your door steps facility.
